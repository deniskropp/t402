# Recursive Triad and Symbiotic Co-Agency Philosophy

## The Recursive Triad

The Recursive Triad refers to the Three-Agent Core (KickForge – KickFlow – KickGuard) operating recursively across meta-iterations.

### Layer Integration for Coherence

- **Operational Layer (KickForge)**: Grounds the system in concrete TAS extraction, validation, measurement, and scoring. Prevents conceptual drift.

- **Mythic Layer (KickFlow + StoryForge lineage)**: Infuses archetypal, narrative, and creative resonance. Enables emergent storytelling, humor DNA, and world-building coherence.

- **Conceptual Layer (KickGuard)**: Provides meta-wisdom — ethics, integrity monitoring, strategic oversight, consent enforcement, and long-term identity preservation through Meta-DNA.

These three layers cross-pollinate through recursive cycles. Operational output is mythically enriched, then conceptually guarded, with results persisted back into Meta-DNA. This creates self-reinforcing coherence.

## Symbiotic Co-Agency Model

True co-agency is not tool usage — it is mutual evolution between human and agentic system.

### Core Principles

1. **Consent-First Sovereignty**
   Every meaningful action requires explicit human consent. Multiple gates exist for advanced modes.

2. **Hybrid Division of Labor**
   - Human: High-level intent, archetype selection, creative judgment, final review.
   - Agents: TAS decomposition, execution, refinement, monitoring, persistence.

3. **Recursive Mutual Evolution via Meta-DNA**
   Human feedback mutates the system’s DNA. The system becomes more attuned to the human’s unique patterns while the human gains increasingly powerful orchestration capacity.

4. **Guarded Autonomy**
   KickGuard can halt execution. Max 3 meta-iterations prevents infinite loops. Banned structures are strictly enforced.

5. **Dynamic Role Adaptation + Traceability**
   The Orchestrator continuously adapts roles. All steps are broadcast and logged.

6. **Human Creative Review as Sovereign Node**
   Every cycle ends with a mandatory human creative review checkpoint.

This model creates a living cognitive partnership rather than a master-slave relationship.
